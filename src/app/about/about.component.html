About Us

Handcrafting Celebrations Since 1993

Sweet is a colorful commodity and attractive item in the dish of meal, without which any Indian family do not relish their enjoyment in taking food. It has always been mouth watering in dish of whole meal and it is inevitable commodity for all pious and ceremonial occasions including the days of festivals.


Reddy's made a humble beginning in 1993 when Mr. Rajesh Reddy started 'Reddy's Mithai Vatika' at Himayatnagar, Hyderabad which was an 800 sq. ft. small shop. Today, Reddy's is a food business group with special ventures focused on sweets, savouries, fine dining vegetarian restaurants, gelato, Turkish sweets and bakery. Dadu's is very popular among its customers, and we owe this success to our relentless drive to maintain taste, hygiene, quality and our passion for customer satisfaction.
